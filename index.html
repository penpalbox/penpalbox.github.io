The Return of the Letter

Nothing beats that moment of excitement when you open the letter box and see a beautifully decorated envelope peering out. Have this moment every month when Pen Pal Box delivers a carefully curated selection of kawaii stationery.

Each month receive cute letter sets, stickers, stamps and embellishments to decorate your pen pal letters. We'll include enough samples for you to send to your pen pals and to keep for yourself so you won't face the struggle of to keep or share again.

Snail Mail Love

We love stationery and sending pen pal letters too so you can be sure the items you receive will be items you'll love to use. We source our quality stationery from all over the world from commercial and independent stationers and artists.
